# PDR-0001 — Nex Ecosystem Identity

## Summary

This document defines the identity, philosophy and scope of the Nex ecosystem.

It establishes the principles that every current and future Nex product must follow.

This is the highest-level Product Decision Record in the repository.

---

# Why This Exists

As software ecosystems grow, products often become inconsistent in quality, design and engineering practices.

The purpose of Nex is to provide a common foundation that ensures every product shares the same philosophy while maintaining its own identity.

Nex is not a single application.

Nex is a long-term ecosystem.

---

# Decision

"Nex" is the master brand for a family of thoughtfully designed digital products.

Every application developed under the Nex name shares the same engineering philosophy, product quality standards and visual identity while maintaining its own purpose.

Every Nex product should feel unique, but immediately recognizable as part of the same ecosystem.

---

# Mission

Create digital experiences that bring people together through thoughtful design, quality engineering and memorable interactions.

---

# Vision

Build an ecosystem of digital products recognized for consistency, craftsmanship and long-term quality.

---

# Core Principles

## People First

Technology exists to improve human experiences.

Every feature must provide meaningful value.

---

## Quality Over Speed

Long-term quality is always more important than delivering features quickly.

---

## Design With Purpose

Every interface element must have a reason to exist.

Visual noise should be avoided.

---

## Engineering For The Future

Every architectural decision should consider future scalability and maintainability.

---

## Documentation Is Part Of The Product

Documentation is considered a core product asset.

Every important decision must be documented.

---

# Brand Identity

Every official Nex product must:

- Start with the **Nex** prefix.
- Follow the Nex Development System (NDS).
- Share the ecosystem visual language.
- Preserve its own personality and purpose.

---

# Visual Identity

The hexagon is the official symbol of the Nex ecosystem.

It represents three fundamental concepts:

- Structure
- Connection
- Growth

Each product may reinterpret the interior of the hexagon while preserving the external shape.

---

# Current Products

## NexPlay

The flagship product of the Nex ecosystem.

Designed to create memorable shared experiences through games.

---

# Future Products

The ecosystem is intentionally open to future applications, including but not limited to:

- NexMaintenance
- NexFinance
- NexHome
- NexFitness

---

# Scope

This document defines the Nex ecosystem only.

Individual applications are responsible for defining their own product vision, architecture and implementation details through dedicated documentation.

---

# Success Criteria

A product belongs to the Nex ecosystem only if it follows the principles established in this document.
